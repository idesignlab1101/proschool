ABOUT PRO SCHOOL

PRO school edu-curation platform started in 2017 by corporate education experts with 10 years of experience.

Our mission is to overcome the limitations of the education industry by providing an open edu-curation platform with future - proof education contents and token economic designs as a method to reinforce life-long learning.

VALUES

  • Incentivized Learning Experience

    PRO school learning Community can earn token incentives and learn at the same time. Your time and efforts are rewarded.

  • Learning Optimized Crypto UI/UX Design

    PRO school platform partnered with crypto payment and off-chain wallet provider to create a seamless reward and payment experience for new users.

  • Self-Expanding Eco-System

    PRO school token is rewarded on class referrals and community engagement. Expanding learning community will attract more educators and learners.
